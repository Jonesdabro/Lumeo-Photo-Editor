Lumeo Photo Editor

Upload and edit your photos directly in your browser with Lumeo!  A versatile photo editor built with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

Features
-Real-time brightness, contrast, saturation etc. adjustments
- Upload your own images
- Download edited photos instantly
- Fully browser-based, no server required

How to Use
1. Open [GitHub Pages link] in your browser
2. Upload a photo
3. Adjust sliders for brightness, contrast, and saturation
4. Click "Download Image" to save your edits
5. Enjoy your polished photos!
